Peanut Butter Sandwich

Home

Description

Jelly not included


Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Warm Water
  • Roasted Peanuts

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast in warm water
  2. Mix the yeast with the all-purpose flour and salt
  3. Knead 10 mins, and let rise an hour
  4. Shape it into loaf, with a second rise 30 to 45 mins long
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for around 35 mins
  6. Cool completely, then slice
  7. Blend the peanuts in a food processor for 3 to 5 mins until it's creamy
  8. Stop occasionally to scrape the sides

WARNING: The following steps are difficult to follow and failure to do so could lead to an emergency as it involves using a butter knife!!!

  1. Make two slices of the bread
  2. Use the butter knife to spread the peanut butter all over one side of both breads
  3. Mash the breads together, with the sides containing the peanut butter facing each other
