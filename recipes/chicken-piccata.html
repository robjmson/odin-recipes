Kevin's Chicken Piccata

Home

Description

Can you believe Kevin of all people managed to make some very good meals?


Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cauliflower Pasta
  • Lemon Caper Sauce

Steps

  1. Break the chicken strips apart in its pouch
  2. Open the chicken strips pouch and drain the broth
  3. Add chicken to a microwave-safe bowl
  4. Add the sauce and the pasta to the bowl
  5. Microwave on high for 2 to 3 minutes
  6. Remove bowl from microwave and stir
  7. Once stirred, microwave for 2 more minutes
  8. Enjoy!
